Questions
Is this actually free? +

Yes. AllNet is free to download and free to use. No entry fees, no subscriptions, no hidden costs.

Can I really earn money playing basketball? +

Yes. You earn Stars by competing on leaderboards, winning challenges, maintaining streaks, and playing pickup games. Stars convert to Visa gift cards at 1,000 Stars = $1.

How do you prevent fake scores? +

Every 3PT attempt and dunk is recorded in-app with GPS verification. Videos are public on the leaderboard. Anyone can flag a suspicious score, and confirmed fraud results in suspension.

Do I need other players nearby? +

Not for 3-Point Contest or Dunk Contest — you can compete solo at any court, anywhere in the US. Pickup games require other players and are currently live in Orange County and Greater LA.

What makes this different from other basketball apps? +

AllNet verifies participation. Your scores are recorded on video, your games are GPS-verified, and your opponents rate you. It's not self-reported stats — it's proven.

How old do I need to be? +

13 or older.
